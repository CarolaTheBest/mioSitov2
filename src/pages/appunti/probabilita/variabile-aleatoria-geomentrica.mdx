come è fatta la PMF?

1. $Imm(X) = \{ 1,2,3,4,5,6,... \}$
2. $\forall k \in IMm(X) = P_x(k) = \mathbb P((X=k))$

$k=1 \quad \mathbb P(X=1)= p$

$k=2 \quad \mathbb P(X=2) = (1-p)\cdot p$

$k=3 \quad \mathbb P(X=3) = (1-p)\cdot (1-p)\cdot p  = (1-p)^2 \cdot p$

$\mathbb P(X=k)=(1-p)^{k-1} \cdot p$

$p = \frac{1}{6} \quad P_X(1)= \frac{1}{6}$

$P_X(2) = \frac{1}{6} \cdot \frac{5}{6} =...$
